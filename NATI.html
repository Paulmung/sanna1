NORTH AMERICA TEEKLUI FAMILY CONSTITUTION

(North America Teeklui Innkuan Thukhunpi)

Khen 1 na

Hih kipawlna min pen “North America Teeklui Family” ki ci ding a, Zopau in “North America Teeklui Innkuan” kici ding hi.

Khenno 2: Hih kipawlna ii “Logo” A kual (circle) khat bawl ding a, Hih akual (circle) pen kipumkhat, kipawl khop(unity) na deih na hi. A kual tungah “North America Teeklui Innkuan” cih kigelh ding hi. A kualpi sung lam ah inn buk sung aa, numei leh pasal ansu om ding aa, tua pen Innkuan khat leh Zo ngeina lah na hi. a nuai pen ah N.A.T.I kipat kum ESTD2010 kikoih ding hi.

Khen 3 na

Hih kipawlna ii “Ciaptehna/seal” pen a nuai a bang hi ding hi. A kual (circle) khat bawl ding a, a kual tungah ah “Nort America Teeklui Innkuan” cih kigelh ding hi. A kual pi sung lam ah inn buk sung aa, numei leh pasal ansu om ding aa, a nuai pen ah N.A.T.I kipat kum ESTD2010 kikoih ding hi. NATI hong pian khiat kum lah na a hihi.
